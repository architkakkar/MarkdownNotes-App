# Welcome to Markdown Notes App!

## Introduction

Welcome to the **Markdown Notes App**, your go-to tool for managing and organizing your notes in Markdown format. This app is designed to make it easy for you to create, edit, and store your notes in a clean, structured format that's perfect for documentation, project planning, and more.

## Getting Started

To get started, simply click on the "+" button to create a new note. You can then start writing your note in Markdown format.
